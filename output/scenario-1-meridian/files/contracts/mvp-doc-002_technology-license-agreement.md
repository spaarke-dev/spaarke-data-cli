**Agreement No.:** TLA-2023-0032
**Effective Date:** March 1, 2023

---

## PARTIES

This Technology License Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of the Effective Date by and between:

**MERIDIAN CORPORATION**, a Delaware corporation, with its principal place of business at 4500 Technology Drive, Suite 300, San Jose, California 95134 ("Licensor" or "Meridian"),

and

**PINNACLE INDUSTRIES, INC.**, a California corporation, with its principal place of business at 1200 Innovation Boulevard, Fremont, California 94538 ("Licensee" or "Pinnacle").

## RECITALS

WHEREAS, Meridian is the owner by assignment of United States Patent No. 9,876,543, entitled "Multi-Stage Thermal Compression Molding System and Method for Advanced Composite Materials" (the "'543 Patent"), issued on June 14, 2022, and all related intellectual property;

WHEREAS, Meridian has developed substantial trade secret know-how relating to the implementation and optimization of the thermal compression molding technology described in the '543 Patent (collectively with the '543 Patent, the "Licensed Technology");

WHEREAS, Pinnacle desires to obtain a limited license to use certain aspects of the Licensed Technology in connection with its manufacturing operations for specific applications, subject to the terms and conditions set forth herein;

WHEREAS, the Parties have entered into a Master Services Agreement dated January 15, 2023 ("MSA"), and this Agreement is intended to supplement and complement the MSA;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants and agreements herein contained, and the payment of the license fees described herein, the Parties agree as follows:

---

## ARTICLE 1: DEFINITIONS

**1.1** "Authorized Facility" means Pinnacle's manufacturing facility located at 1200 Innovation Boulevard, Fremont, California 94538, and such other facilities as may be approved in writing by Meridian from time to time.

**1.2** "Confidential Information" has the meaning assigned to it in the MSA.

**1.3** "Field of Use" means the manufacture of precision-molded composite components for use exclusively in the automotive heat exchanger market segment, limited to components with a maximum dimension not exceeding 450 millimeters in any axis.

**1.4** "Licensed Patents" means US Patent No. 9,876,543, together with all continuations, continuations-in-part, divisionals, reissues, reexaminations, and extensions thereof, and all foreign counterparts.

**1.5** "Licensed Technology" means the Licensed Patents and the Licensed Know-How, collectively.

**1.6** "Licensed Know-How" means Meridian's proprietary and confidential technical information, trade secrets, processes, procedures, formulations, specifications, and other know-how related to the thermal compression molding technology described in the Licensed Patents, as set forth in Exhibit A (Technical Package).

**1.7** "Licensed Products" means products manufactured by Pinnacle within the Field of Use using the Licensed Technology at the Authorized Facility.

**1.8** "Net Sales" means the gross revenue received by Pinnacle from the sale of Licensed Products to unaffiliated third parties, less: (a) trade discounts and allowances actually granted; (b) credits for returns; (c) freight, insurance, and shipping charges; and (d) sales, use, and value-added taxes actually paid.

**1.9** "Royalty Term" means the period commencing on the Effective Date and continuing until the later of: (a) the expiration of the last-to-expire Licensed Patent; or (b) ten (10) years from the Effective Date.

## ARTICLE 2: GRANT OF LICENSE

**2.1 License Grant.** Subject to the terms and conditions of this Agreement, Meridian hereby grants to Pinnacle a non-exclusive, non-transferable, limited license under the Licensed Technology to:

(a) Use the Licensed Technology solely within the Field of Use;
(b) Manufacture Licensed Products at the Authorized Facility;
(c) Sell Licensed Products to Pinnacle's customers in the Territory;
(d) Use the Licensed Know-How in connection with the foregoing activities.

**2.2 Territory.** The license granted herein is limited to the United States of America and Canada (the "Territory").

**2.3 Restrictions.** The license granted herein is subject to the following restrictions:

(a) **Field of Use Limitation.** Pinnacle shall use the Licensed Technology exclusively within the Field of Use and shall not apply the technology to any other product category, market segment, or application without the prior written consent of Meridian;

(b) **Facility Limitation.** Pinnacle shall practice the Licensed Technology only at the Authorized Facility;

(c) **No Sublicensing.** Pinnacle shall not sublicense, assign, or transfer the license or any rights thereunder to any third party without the prior written consent of Meridian;

(d) **No Modification.** Pinnacle shall not modify, adapt, improve, or create derivative works of the Licensed Technology without the prior written consent of Meridian. Any improvements developed by Pinnacle in violation of this restriction shall be the sole property of Meridian;

(e) **No Reverse Engineering.** Pinnacle shall not reverse engineer, decompile, or otherwise attempt to derive the source technology underlying the Licensed Know-How beyond what is expressly provided in the Technical Package;

(f) **Volume Limitation.** Annual production volume of Licensed Products shall not exceed 500,000 units without renegotiation of the royalty rates set forth in Article 3.

**2.4 Reservation of Rights.** Meridian reserves all rights not expressly granted herein, including the right to: (a) practice the Licensed Technology itself; (b) grant licenses to other parties; and (c) enforce the Licensed Patents against infringers.

**2.5 No Implied License.** Nothing in this Agreement shall be construed as granting any rights by implication, estoppel, or otherwise to any intellectual property of Meridian other than the Licensed Technology as expressly defined herein.

## ARTICLE 3: FINANCIAL TERMS

**3.1 Upfront License Fee.** Pinnacle shall pay Meridian a non-refundable, non-creditable upfront license fee of Five Hundred Thousand Dollars ($500,000.00) within thirty (30) days of the Effective Date.

**3.2 Running Royalty.** During the Royalty Term, Pinnacle shall pay Meridian a running royalty equal to:

(a) **Standard Rate:** Four percent (4.0%) of Net Sales of Licensed Products;
(b) **Reduced Rate:** Three percent (3.0%) of Net Sales of Licensed Products in excess of Ten Million Dollars ($10,000,000.00) in any calendar year;
(c) **Volume Discount:** Two and one-half percent (2.5%) of Net Sales of Licensed Products in excess of Twenty-Five Million Dollars ($25,000,000.00) in any calendar year.

**3.3 Minimum Annual Royalty.** Commencing in the second year of the Royalty Term, Pinnacle shall pay Meridian a minimum annual royalty of One Hundred Fifty Thousand Dollars ($150,000.00) per calendar year ("Minimum Royalty"). Running royalties paid in a given calendar year shall be credited against the Minimum Royalty for such year. Any shortfall between running royalties and the Minimum Royalty shall be due and payable within thirty (30) days of the end of the applicable calendar year.

**3.4 Royalty Payments.** Royalty payments shall be due quarterly, within forty-five (45) days after the end of each calendar quarter (March 31, June 30, September 30, and December 31). Each payment shall be accompanied by a written report setting forth, for the applicable quarter:

(a) The number and type of Licensed Products manufactured and sold;
(b) The gross revenue and Net Sales of Licensed Products;
(c) The calculation of royalties due;
(d) Any adjustments or credits applied.

**3.5 Audit Rights.** Meridian shall have the right, upon thirty (30) days' prior written notice and no more than once per calendar year, to audit Pinnacle's books and records related to Licensed Products. Such audit shall be conducted by an independent certified public accountant selected by Meridian and reasonably acceptable to Pinnacle, during normal business hours at Pinnacle's offices. If the audit reveals an underpayment of royalties of five percent (5%) or more for any audited period, Pinnacle shall bear the cost of the audit and pay the deficiency plus interest at the rate of one and one-half percent (1.5%) per month.

**3.6 Late Payments.** All past-due amounts shall bear interest at the lesser of one and one-half percent (1.5%) per month or the maximum rate permitted by applicable law.

## ARTICLE 4: TECHNOLOGY TRANSFER

**4.1 Technical Package.** Within sixty (60) days of the Effective Date, Meridian shall deliver to Pinnacle a Technical Package consisting of:

(a) Detailed process specifications for the thermal compression molding process;
(b) Equipment specifications and recommended configurations;
(c) Quality control procedures and testing protocols;
(d) Training materials and operational guidelines;
(e) Such other technical information as reasonably necessary for Pinnacle to practice the Licensed Technology within the Field of Use.

**4.2 Training.** Meridian shall provide up to forty (40) hours of on-site training to Pinnacle's personnel at the Authorized Facility, at no additional charge. Additional training shall be available at Meridian's then-current consulting rates as set forth in the MSA.

**4.3 Technical Support.** During the first twelve (12) months following the Effective Date, Meridian shall provide reasonable technical support to Pinnacle in connection with the implementation of the Licensed Technology, including telephone and email support during normal business hours (Pacific Time, Monday through Friday, excluding holidays).

**5.3 Infringement Notification.** Each Party shall promptly notify the other Party in writing if it becomes aware of any actual or suspected infringement of the Licensed Patents by a third party, or any claim that the Licensed Technology infringes a third party's intellectual property rights.

**5.4 Enforcement.** Meridian shall have the sole right to enforce the Licensed Patents against third-party infringers. If Meridian elects not to enforce within ninety (90) days of notice, Pinnacle may request permission to enforce at its own expense, and Meridian shall not unreasonably withhold such permission.

**5.5 Improvements.** Any improvements, modifications, or enhancements to the Licensed Technology made by Pinnacle during the term of this Agreement ("Improvements") shall be:

(a) Promptly disclosed to Meridian in writing;
(b) Assigned to Meridian, with Pinnacle hereby assigning all right, title, and interest in such Improvements to Meridian;
(c) Licensed back to Pinnacle under the same terms as the Licensed Technology for the duration of this Agreement.

## ARTICLE 7: TERM AND TERMINATION

**7.1 Term.** This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and shall continue for the duration of the Royalty Term, unless earlier terminated in accordance with this Article 7.

**7.2 Termination for Breach.** Either Party may terminate this Agreement upon sixty (60) days' written notice if the other Party materially breaches this Agreement and fails to cure such breach within the notice period.

**7.3 Termination for Non-Payment.** Meridian may terminate this Agreement upon thirty (30) days' written notice if Pinnacle fails to pay any amounts due hereunder and does not cure such failure within the notice period.

**7.4 Termination for Patent Challenge.** Meridian may terminate this Agreement immediately upon written notice if Pinnacle directly or indirectly challenges the validity or enforceability of any Licensed Patent.

**7.5 Effect of Termination.** Upon termination or expiration:

(a) All rights and licenses granted to Pinnacle shall immediately cease;
(b) Pinnacle shall cease all use of the Licensed Technology within thirty (30) days;
(c) Pinnacle shall return or destroy all copies of the Licensed Know-How and Technical Package;
(d) Pinnacle shall pay all royalties accrued through the date of termination;
(e) Pinnacle may sell off existing inventory of Licensed Products for a period of ninety (90) days, subject to payment of applicable royalties;
(f) Sections 5.1, 5.5, 6.3, and Article 8 shall survive termination.
